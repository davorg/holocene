Why the Holocene Calendar?

The Holocene Calendar (Human Era) simply adds 10,000 years to the current Gregorian year. This places the start of the calendar at approximately 10,000 BCE — the beginning of large-scale human civilisation.

Technical Benefits

  • No BCE/CE split — all years are positive
  • No missing year zero
  • Simpler chronological calculations

Psychological Benefits

  • Frames history as a continuous human story
  • Removes reliance on a single cultural/religious reference point
  • Emphasises long-term thinking (we are in year 12000+, not 2000+)

It’s a minimal change with a surprisingly large shift in perspective.
